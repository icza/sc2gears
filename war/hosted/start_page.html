October 7, 2015 - Patch 3.0 - The END of Sc2gears

StarCraft II's Patch 3.0 rendered Sc2gears' parsing engine incapable of doing its work. In short: Sc2gears can no longer parse replays since Patch 3.0.

No further updates will be released for Sc2gears, only Scelight will be maintained. Visit the Scelight home page and start using it.

✞ R.I.P. Sc2gears! Lived 5 years, 6 months and 5 days! (April 2, 2010 - October 7, 2015)

May God bless You All.

July 15, 2015 - The Sc2gears Database is shutting down

The Sc2gears Database is shutting down. As of now, no new accounts can be created.

On July 28th, 2015 starting at 12pm Pacific Time, the Sc2gears Database will be temporarily shut down for 24 hours.
On August 4th, 2015 the Sc2gears Database will be permanently shut down at 12:00pm Pacific time.

The reason for shutting down the Sc2gears Database is because it is built using a service of the Google App Engine Platform that will be shut down at the mentioned times.

There will be no more Sc2gears releases. Currently Sc2gears supports only WoL and HotS, and it doesn't support LotV. And this will remain this way.
Most likely when LotV is released, Sc2gears will stop functioning for all replays (including WoL, HotS and LotV) due to replay format changes.

The Quota of the Free Sc2gears Database package has been decreased from 50 MB to 10 5 MB.

April 4 - Sc2gears turns 3!

Yet another year has passed, Sc2gears celebrates its 3rd birthday!

In the last year (since the 2nd birthday) 17 major releases of Sc2gears were published; 21 including minor releases (that's about 1 major release in every 3 weeks!).

As a reminder, the statistics for the 2nd year: 27 major releases, 36 including minor releases. And in the first year: 21 major releases, 31 including minor releases.
This adds up to a total of: 65 major releases; 88 including minor releases!

Last year also brought us Sharing and Team support in the Sc2gears Database, and the Sc2gears Database has been opened to everyone by presenting a tiny registration form on the User Page to those without an account requiring only 30 seconds to get started with a free account.
Last year is also the beginning of a new era: Heart of the Swarm. Sc2gears has been equipped to fully support HotS replays and changes that came with HotS.
And last but not least the long awaited feature, the Historical league info has been added and integrated everywhere in Sc2gears. Sc2gears can display the leagues of players at the time of the game, and you can also filter games by league match-up and see league statistics in the Multi-replay analysis.

Thanks Everyone for the continuous support and feedback!

Despite the long history and success of Sc2gears, most players still don't know about it.
Please help by spreading the word. Let your friends know about Sc2gears. Put links on your sites and blogs linking to the Sc2gears home page:
https://sites.google.com/site/sc2gears/
Thanks.

March 7 - Mouse print image preview in the Sc2gears Database

The Sc2gears Database now supports previewing mouse print images without having to download and open them in Sc2gears.

The Mouse prints page of the Sc2gears Database User Page lists your uploaded mouse prints.
The Actions column now displays an image icon for each mouse prints whose tool tip says: "Preview mouse print image". Clicking on it will open the mouse print image in a dialog window.
The mouse prints table now also has a File size column.

These preview images are generated by the Sc2gears Database, you don't have to upload mouse print images (just the mouse print data files (*.SMPD)).
Also note that these preview images are generated on-the-fly and are not stored, so they don't use your storage quota.
